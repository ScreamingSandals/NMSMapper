Mapping Browser Discontinued

As of May 30th, 2024, we have shut down this mapping browser in favor of a new and improved version hosted at mappings.dev. The new browser includes features such as type generics, client mappings, enhanced history comparison, faster build times, and more.

Discontinuation Notice

In June 2021, with the release of Minecraft 1.17, Spigot's team decided to abandon their member mappings in favor of a Mojang-mapped development environment with an obfuscated runtime. This change was unexpected for us, and because we needed to access NMS in our plugins, we created this project. It enabled us to browse mappings, track the history of individual symbols, and automatically generate reflective accessors for multiple versions at once. However, the project has faced significant issues, primarily high memory usage and poor performance, which have worsened with each new version.

Therefore, one of our team members developed a new project called  takenaka. This new successor to NMSMapper has many new features, such as support for viewing generics and parameter names. Most importantly, it performs much better than NMSMapper. As a result, we have decided to retire NMSMapper in favor of takenaka.

Mapping browser
The mapping browser is no longer supported. We have implemented redirects on our website, so all links to specific versions, packages, and classes now point to mappings.dev. Most existing links should function correctly, except for those related to history comparisons of individual classes.

Reflective accessor generator
If you used NMSMapper to generate reflective accessors, you will need to update your build scripts and source code to work with takenaka. We understand this may be time-consuming, especially for larger build scripts. Therefore, we will continue updating NMSMapper's generator on demand. If you require an update, please request it on our Discord server or via GitHub.

Migrating from Spigot to Mojang mappings?

Mojang <-> Spigot comparison pages are now available. On these pages, you can see all Spigot-mapped symbols and their Mojang equivalents.

These symbols are compared across multiple versions:

  • 1.16.5 - the last Spigot version with Spigot-mapped fields
  • 1.17.1 - the last Spigot version with Spigot-mapped methods
  • 1.18.2 - the first major Spigot version in which only classes are Spigot-mapped

Symbols which weren't Spigot-mapped in 1.16.5 are not compared.

Note: These pages may negatively impact your browser's performance, due to their size and processing power needed to render them.
